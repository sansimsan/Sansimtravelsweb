Toronto

City of Skyscrapers and Lakes

Toronto, Canada’s most vibrant and multicultural city, invites travelers to experience a captivating blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty. Known for its iconic skyline, crowned by the CN Tower, Toronto is a place where diverse neighborhoods thrive and cultures converge. From the historic Distillery District’s cobblestone streets to the bustling energy of Yonge-Dundas Square, every corner of the city has its own unique charm. Explorers can wander through world-class museums, including the Royal Ontario Museum and the Art Gallery of Ontario, or dive into the culinary delights of St. Lawrence Market, where flavors from around the globe come together. Beyond downtown, visitors can enjoy tranquil moments on Toronto Islands, offering stunning views of the cityscape, or venture out to explore High Park’s scenic trails and gardens. Toronto also boasts a thriving arts and entertainment scene, from Broadway-caliber theater in the Entertainment District to lively festivals celebrating music, film, and more. With a welcoming spirit and a calendar filled with events, Toronto provides an enriching travel experience for all seasons and all interests. Discover why this city is cherished not only for its skyline but for the vibrant communities and hidden gems that make it unforgettable. Whether you're a foodie, history buff, or nature lover, Toronto offers countless ways to connect, explore, and be inspired.

Restaurants

Flavourful Journey

Alo

Consistently ranked as one of Canada’s best restaurants, Alo is a French fine-dining spot with an ever-changing tasting menu. The dining experience is known for its impeccable service and creative dishes.

Richmond Station

A Canadian bistro by Chef Carl Heinrich, this restaurant focuses on farm-to-table fare with a frequently changing menu. Known for its fresh ingredients and exceptional service, it’s especially famous for its burger and chef’s tasting menu.

Bar Isabel

Inspired by Spanish tapas bars, Bar Isabel offers a lively ambiance with a delicious menu of shareable plates like grilled octopus, charcuterie, and seafood. It’s perfect for a night out with friends.

Scaramouche Restaurant & Pasta Bar

A Toronto classic with stunning views of the city skyline, Scaramouche offers an upscale dining experience with a focus on contemporary French cuisine. Its Coconut Cream Pie is a must-try dessert.
